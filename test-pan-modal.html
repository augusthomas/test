How Tellus Interest Works

When is interest calculated?

Interest is calculated at midnight PST daily.

When do I start receiving interest?

You start receiving interest at midnight following a 24 hour processing period. For example, if you have made an investment at 3:00PM on Tuesday, you will start receiving interest at 12:00AM on Thursday.

How is interest calculated?

Interest is calculated based on your investment amount at midnight. This does not include amounts still in the processing period.
